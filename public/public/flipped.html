Flipped

Release date: 2010
Genre: Comedy/Romance
Length: 1h 30m

Description

The story begins with Julie Baker falling in love with her neighbour, Bryce Loski, however, Bryce is repulsed by her. This continues from childhood to teenagehood. Eventually Bryce offends her to the point wherer she no longer likes him. But Bryce has justed started to catch feelings for her. Some would say he has flipped.
